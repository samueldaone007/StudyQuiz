About StudyQuiz

Empowering students with AI-powered learning tools

Our Mission

StudyQuiz is designed to revolutionize the way students learn and study. We believe that every student deserves a personalized learning experience that adapts to their unique needs and learning style.

Our platform combines the power of Artificial Intelligence with proven learning techniques to help students:

  • Save time by automatically summarizing lengthy study materials
  • Focus on key concepts that matter most
  • Reinforce learning through personalized quizzes
  • Track progress and identify areas for improvement

The Technology

StudyQuiz leverages advanced Natural Language Processing (NLP) techniques to:

AI Summarization

Extractive and abstractive summarization to condense study materials

Keyword Extraction

Identify and extract important concepts from text

Quiz Generation

Automatically create relevant questions from content

Progress Tracking

Monitor learning outcomes and performance

Research Foundation

This project is based on extensive research in educational technology and learning science, including:

  • Constructivist Learning Theory - Learning through active engagement and knowledge construction
  • Adaptive Learning Theory - Dynamic adjustment of content based on individual performance
  • Testing Effect - Improved long-term retention through regular self-testing
  • Personalized Learning - Tailoring educational experiences to individual learner needs

AI API Integration

StudyQuiz uses AI to generate intelligent quiz questions. You can choose from multiple AI providers:

Provider Quality Cost Setup
OpenAI GPT-3.5 Best Paid (low cost) Add OPENAI_API_KEY to .env
Hugging Face Good Free tier Add HUGGINGFACE_API_TOKEN to .env
Local AI (Default) Standard Free No setup needed
Setup Instructions:
  1. Copy .env.example to .env
  2. Add your API key(s) to the .env file
  3. Restart the Django server
  4. Generate quizzes with AI-powered questions!
